📊 ATC Score Explanation

ATC score helps evaluate animal quality.

  • 🟢 85–100: Excellent
  • 🟡 70–84: Good
  • 🟠 55–69: Medium
  • 🔴 40–54: Poor
  • ⚫ Below 40: Very Poor
